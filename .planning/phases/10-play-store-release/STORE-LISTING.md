## Full Description

Zenith Grid is Tic Tac Toe, reimagined for Android.

**PLAY FRIENDS OVER LOCAL WI-FI**
No accounts. No internet. Just two devices on the same Wi-Fi — and you're playing in seconds. Automatic device discovery means no IP address to type, no setup to fumble through. Tap "Host", your friend taps "Join", and the game begins.

**CHALLENGE AN UNBEATABLE AI**
Think you can win? Three difficulty levels: Easy lets beginners find their footing, Medium plays smart but makes mistakes, and Hard runs a perfect Minimax algorithm — it never loses. Use it to sharpen your game, or prove to yourself that a tie is the best outcome.

**PASS AND PLAY WITH FAMILY**
Hand the phone to a friend or family member and take turns on the same screen. Simple, instant, no fuss. Perfect for a quick game anywhere.

**THE DETAILS**
• Animated win line drawn across the board
• Spring-based piece placement animations
• Sound effects for placement, win, and draw
• Session score tracker across multiple rounds
• Full dark and light mode support
• No ads. No in-app purchases. Ever.
